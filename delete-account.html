Delete Your Account - My Tennis Coach

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly within the My Tennis Coach app:

  1. Open the app
  2. Go to Profile (bottom right tab)
  3. Scroll down to Account section
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Type "DELETE" (or "SUPPRIMER" in French) to confirm
  6. Your account and all data will be permanently deleted

What Gets Deleted

  • Your email and account credentials
  • All tennis session history
  • Game plan and tactics
  • Match day checklist customizations
  • Personal mantras
  • All AI coaching conversations

Data Retention

No data is retained after account deletion. All information is permanently deleted from our servers immediately.
